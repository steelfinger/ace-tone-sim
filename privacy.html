Privacy Policy

Rhythm Ace · Last updated April 26, 2026

No data collected

Rhythm Ace does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information. No account is required to use the app.

No third-party services

The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries of any kind.

Microphone permission

iOS requires apps that use the audio engine to declare microphone access. Rhythm Ace uses this permission solely to initialise the audio playback system. The app does not record, store, or transmit any audio from your microphone.
